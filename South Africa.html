SOUTH AFRICA

South Africa has been a democratic republic since holding it's first open election on April 27, 1994.
Natural resources, agriculture, tourism, and manufacturing have made South Africa the largest economy on the continent.
But problems with unemployment, poverty, and AIDS present huge challenges for the government.

Many different peoples make up South Africa, each with their own language and history. The country has 11 official and many more unofficial ones.This colorful mix of cultures gives South Africa its nickname"rainbow nation"
