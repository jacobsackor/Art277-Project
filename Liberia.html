LIBERIA

The republic of liberia is one of the poorliest countries in the world, partly because of the 14 years civil war that ended in 1997, which has continued to impact its population of 4.9 million people
Life in liberia is potray by the living conditions of its citizens, one key key hindrance to good life in liberia is poverty

Half of Liberians live in adject poverty, this means that they are severely deprived of their basics needs, such as food, clean water, shelter, sanitation, and health care access. The 14 years civil war in Liberia caused a breakdown in law enforcement which has also made room for Corruption within the country, other than this, Liberia is a country blessed with many natural resources
